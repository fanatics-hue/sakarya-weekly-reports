Pipe / Plate Steel Damage
Visual & Dimensional — Plate Entrance / Station 1
During Visual and Dimensional Inspection at plate entrance and Dimensional Station 1, a deep internal surface mark was noted on the base metal — around mid pipe length, opposite the weld bead, ~180° from the weld seam, depth 3–5 mm. Identified as caused by the “U” press, from a foreign material melted onto the press surface. Two further mechanical-damage types noted: a “bolt” aspect and stamp damage on the ID pipe surface.
